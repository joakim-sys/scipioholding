We invest in talented business professionals and support them as they search for a company to acquire and subsequently run. Scipio is a serialacquirer of small and medium-sized family-owned businesses. In this way, Scipio takes advantage of the growing global trend SMEs that face succession issues to acquire attractive assets at extraordinary prices. Scipio invests globally based on the idea that it seeks to invest in the best-talent wherever they reside.
